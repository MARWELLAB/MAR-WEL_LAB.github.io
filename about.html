About MAR-WEL LAB

Marine Aquaculture, Risers & Wave Energy Laboratory — DOE, IIT Madras

Who We Are

MAR-WEL LAB is a research group in the Department of Ocean Engineering (DOE) at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras). We focus on advanced experimental, numerical, and analytical studies in three interconnected domains of ocean engineering.

Our research spans from fundamental hydrodynamics to applied engineering solutions — bridging the gap between cutting-edge science and real-world marine industry challenges. We collaborate actively with industry partners, government agencies, and international research institutions.

The lab is equipped with state-of-the-art experimental facilities including wave flumes, towing tanks, and structural testing rigs that enable high-fidelity physical model testing.

Our Four Pillars

The MAR-WEL name represents our three core research domains.

🐟
MAR — Marine Aquaculture

Offshore cage systems, mooring, structural response under wave & current loading.

🔧
Risers

VIV analysis, fatigue life assessment, and structural integrity of offshore risers.

🌊
WEL — Wave Energy

WEC design, array optimization, physical modelling, and power take-off studies.

🌊🌉
Coastal Protection Structures

Breakwater design, wave attenuation, hydrodynamic stability & coastal protection.

Facilities

World-class experimental infrastructure at DOE, IIT Madras.

🌊

Wave Flume

Multi-directional wave basin for physical model testing of WECs, aquaculture structures, and offshore components.

🚢

Towing Tank

High-speed towing tank for VIV experiments and hydrodynamic characterization of offshore structures.

💻

Numerical Lab

High-performance computing cluster for CFD simulations, structural FEA, and large-scale numerical modelling.
