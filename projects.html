Projects

Funded research projects and industry collaborations

Ongoing Projects

Active research funded by government agencies and industry partners.

01

Offshore Wave Energy Converter Array Optimization

Design, hydrodynamic analysis, and physical model testing of WEC arrays for optimal energy capture along the Indian coastline. Involves numerical modelling (ANSYS AQWA) and wave flume experiments.

Ministry of Earth Sciences Wave Energy Ongoing
02

Structural Health Monitoring of Marine Risers

Development of SHM frameworks for steel catenary risers subjected to VIV and fatigue loading. Combines experimental data from towing tank tests with machine-learning-based anomaly detection.

ONGC Risers Ongoing
03

Hydrodynamic Assessment of Offshore Fish Cage Systems

Physical and numerical modelling of submersible offshore fish cages under combined wave and current loading. Focus on net deformation, mooring tension, and structural integrity assessment.

Ministry of Fisheries Aquaculture Ongoing

Completed Projects

Successfully delivered research projects.

04

VIV Suppression Devices for Offshore Risers

Experimental investigation of helical strakes and fairings for VIV suppression on flexible riser models. Results provided practical guidelines for offshore operators.

DST Risers Completed
05

Oscillating Water Column WEC — Prototype Development

Design and testing of a scaled OWC prototype for near-shore wave energy extraction. Demonstrated 28% energy conversion efficiency under regular wave conditions.
