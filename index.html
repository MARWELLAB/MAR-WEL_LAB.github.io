Research Focus

Our lab integrates three pillars of ocean engineering to address real-world marine challenges.

🌊

Wave Energy

Developing and testing novel wave energy converter (WEC) devices, hydrodynamic modelling, and physical experiments in our wave flume facilities.

🔧

Marine Risers

Structural analysis, fatigue assessment, and VIV (Vortex-Induced Vibration) studies of offshore riser systems under extreme ocean conditions.

🐟

Marine Aquaculture

Engineering solutions for offshore fish cage systems — structural integrity, mooring design, and hydrodynamic response under wave/current loading.
