Research Areas

Advancing ocean engineering through experimental and numerical research

Wave Energy

Harnessing ocean wave power through innovative WEC technologies.

🌊 Wave Energy Converters (WECs)

  • Design and optimization of oscillating water column (OWC) devices
  • Point absorber and attenuator WEC hydrodynamics
  • Physical model testing in wave flume facilities
  • Power take-off (PTO) system analysis and optimization

⚡ Array Optimization & Grid Integration

  • WEC array layout optimization for maximum power capture
  • Interaction effects and q-factor analysis
  • Numerical modelling using ANSYS AQWA, WAMIT, OpenFOAM
  • Grid integration and power quality studies

Marine Risers

Structural integrity of offshore pipeline and riser systems.

🔧 Vortex-Induced Vibration (VIV)

  • Experimental VIV studies using towing tank facilities
  • Suppression devices: helical strakes, fairings, and grooves
  • Numerical modelling of VIV response using CFD
  • Long flexible riser fatigue under combined loading

🏗️ Structural Analysis & Fatigue

  • Non-linear structural analysis of steel catenary risers (SCR)
  • Fatigue life prediction under irregular wave loading
  • Coupled riser–vessel interaction studies
  • Composite riser material characterization

Marine Aquaculture

Engineering robust offshore fish farming structures.

🐟 Offshore Fish Cage Systems

  • Hydrodynamic response of submersible and surface fish cages
  • Net deformation and volume reduction under current loading
  • Mooring system design and optimization for offshore cages
  • Physical model testing of cage structures in wave and current

⚓ Mooring & Station Keeping

  • Catenary and taut-leg mooring configurations
  • Dynamic mooring line tension analysis
  • Combined wave–current–wind loading on mooring systems
  • Anchor holding capacity in seabed soils
